Electronics

  • Right To Repair

    Repair cafes, makerspaces and open-source hardware can go a long way towards combatting planned obsolescence. I'd like to explore community repair groups and learn how to make the most of electronics I already own.

  • DIY and Single-Board Computers

    It fascinates me what single-board computers and microcontrollers, like the Raspberry Pi and Arduino, can create and automate. I'll be trying some simple kits and projects.

  • Green Computing

    I'm interested in many aspects of green computing, from solar-powered devices, to ethical supply sourcing, and environmentally-conscious tech recycling. I want to integrate these concepts into the things I make.
