About Me

I'm not new to tech, but I am new to taking it seriously as a passion.

I was into computers since I was a kid, but like many folks, I never thought I had what it took for a career in that field. Glad I was wrong! As it turned out, the technical and data aspects of my nonprofit jobs were always what I loved the most. Changing careers in midlife to pursue programming and cyber security has made me think hard about how I'm really interested in spending my days.

It's important to me to keep an ethical, community-first focus, to any tech training I do, and the work it invites. Whether the way we relate to tech is truly at a turning point, or just feels like one, the vast knowledge that's currently available means we each have a powerful impact on what the future could be.
